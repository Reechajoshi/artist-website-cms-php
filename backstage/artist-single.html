Born in 1992 in Calicut City, Jishnu Sudarsan is a professional Music Producer/Songwriter and Vocalist. He emerged in the wake of the influence of Melody with Alternative Rock & Indie Pop. Jishnu was brought up in Jeddah and was deeply passionate in the way of making songs and its programming techniques. He decided to learn the basics of music at the age of 15 by learning to play the keyboard. At the age of 17, Jishnu was a keyboardist in the band based in Jeddah named ‘Clash’ with the base genre of Hip Hop. He was the programmer of the band. However, he soon migrated to the band ‘Black Memories’ as a Music Producer/Music Director. ‘Black Memories’ opened a different door for Jishnu. From Hip Hop, he was interested in creating Smooth Chill and Ambient tracks. His first music composition named ‘Emotions’ was brought out. He did not get a great response, as he was working on Arabian music market and no one would really listen to the English Chill music. However, everything changed once again when he came to India.
